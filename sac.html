Students' Alumni Cell

Over the course of the past few years, Students' Alumni Cell has emerged as a key organ of the Office of Alumni Affairs and International Relations (AA&IR) in executing its yearlong events and activities. Students' Alumni Cell continuously endeavours to strengthen the relations of the alumni of this Institute and their alma mater.
Formed as and operating under the Dean, (AA&IR) , Students' Alumni Cell has been instrumental in organizing the Annual Alumni Meet, Regional Student-Alumni Meets, publishing the quarterly alumni newsletter (KGPian), the annual literary magazine (Yearnings of Yore), the Annual Yearbook and organizing regular guest lectures by distinguished alumni.
Students' Alumni Cell also operates the Student - Alumni Mentorship Programme, My Imprint and organises Alvida (the annual farewell dinner).
